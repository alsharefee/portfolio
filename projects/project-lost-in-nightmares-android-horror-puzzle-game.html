Lost In Nightmares: Android Horror / Puzzle Game

Release Date: 2013

Unity (JavaScript) Android Horror Puzzle

Lost In Nightmares is a special project to me, it marks my very first commercial release on the Google Play Store back in 2013. Developed using Unity3D and JavaScript (AKA UnityScript, was the primary, default scripting language when Unity first launched and during its early years), this first-person horror and puzzle game was a massive learning experience.

The Reception

The game was incredibly well received for a debut solo project, reaching over 70,000+ installations within its first three months. It featured immersive FPS movement, challenging puzzles, and a genuinely tense atmosphere that players loved.

Where is it now?

After remaining on the Play Store for nearly 10 years, Google introduced new requirements to update all apps to support modern Android versions. As I had moved on to newer technologies and projects, I decided not to update the legacy codebase, leading to its eventual removal from the official store.
