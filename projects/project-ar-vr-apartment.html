AR/VR Apartment Marketing

Release Date: 2017

AR VR Apple ARKit Mobile

In 2017, the Virtual Reality landscape was dominated by headsets that relied on cumbersome external sensors—standalone devices like the Oculus Quest didn't exist yet. To overcome this limitation, I developed an innovative solution aimed at the real estate sector. Leveraging the newly released Apple ARKit, I created a sensor-free tracking system that functioned entirely on a mobile device.

The core concept was simple but powerful: a single application that utilizes Augmented Reality to view a property, which can instantly transform into an immersive Virtual Reality experience by splitting the screen for a mobile VR headset. This seamless AR-to-VR transition provided real estate companies with a highly accessible, cutting-edge tool to market their properties without the need for expensive, tethered hardware setups.
