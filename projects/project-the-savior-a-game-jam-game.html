"The" Savior: a Game jam game

Release Date: 2024

Game Jam PC

The Savior introduces a highly unique take on the local co-op genre. In this experimental game, two players must share control of a single hero, representing the conflicting consciences the angel and the devil perched upon her shoulders. Who wins? Who loses? You decide her fate.

This chaotic control scheme requires two separate controllers, utilizing the triggers and thumbsticks to maneuver the character and influence her actions. It's a hilarious and thought provoking experience that tests coordination and communication between players in an entirely new way.
